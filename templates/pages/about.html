About shopifygmc

shopifygmc is a continuous Google Merchant Center compliance auditor for Shopify stores. We catch the things that suspend accounts — missing GTINs, broken product schema, policy-violation copy, image quality — before Google does.

The product is run by SmartShopAutomation, a small team based in Casablanca and serving merchants worldwide. We built it after watching too many Shopify stores get blindsided by Merchant Center suspensions that took weeks to undo.

What we believe

  • Monitoring beats remediation. Daily audits with diffs catch regressions the same day they ship — a theme update, a new app, a bulk-edit gone wrong.
  • AI is best at suggesting, not deciding. Every fix recommendation includes the source rule + the change we'd make, so a human still calls the shot.
  • Price honestly. $12/month covers a real Shopify store with weekly checks. No "starting from" footnotes, no upsell tunnels.
